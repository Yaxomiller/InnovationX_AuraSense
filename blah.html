About Us

At AuraSense, we are dedicated to revolutionizing agriculture and consumer health through innovative technology. Our project aims to develop a handheld device utilizing Raspberry Pi for real-time plant disease detection for farmers and vegetable quality detection for consumers.

The device will capture images of plant leaves and employ deep learning techniques to analyze them. Using convolutional neural networks (CNNs), the system will classify the leaves as healthy or diseased. If diseased, it will further provide recommendations on what fertilizer to use.

We're committed to ensuring food safety and sustainability. That's why our team is dedicated to training the model on a dataset of labeled images encompassing various plant diseases, enabling accurate detection and informed decision-making for farmers.

In addition, our solution includes a section where buyers can capture images of vegetables to predict their freshness and health status, empowering consumers to make informed purchasing decisions and promote healthier lifestyles.
